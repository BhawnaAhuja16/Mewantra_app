Who We Are

Founded in 2022, Mewantra began with a simple mission: to bring the finest quality dry fruits and nuts directly to consumers at reasonable prices.

We believe that high-quality dry fruits should be accessible to everyone. That's why we've established direct relationships with premium farmers and suppliers to ensure we deliver only the freshest and most delicious products to your doorstep.

Our name "Mewantra" combines "Mewa" (the Hindi term for dry fruits) and "Mantra" (a sacred utterance), symbolizing our dedication to treating dry fruits with the respect and care they deserve.
