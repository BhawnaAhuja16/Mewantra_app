Our Heritage

A tale of passion, perseverance, and a commitment to excellence.

Mewantra was born from a family tradition that spans three generations. Our founder, Vikram Singh, grew up in a household where his grandfather, a respected dry fruit merchant in Old Delhi, taught him the art of distinguishing premium produce by texture, aroma, and appearance.

In 2022, after working with international exporters and witnessing firsthand the journey of dry fruits from orchard to market, Vikram established Mewantra with a vision to bridge the gap between discerning consumers and exceptional produce.

Our name "Mewantra" combines "Mewa" (the Hindi term for dry fruits) and "Mantra" (a sacred utterance), symbolizing our dedication to treating dry fruits with the respect and reverence they deserve.

Our Quality Commitment

At Mewantra, quality isn't just a promise—it's our foundation. We've implemented a rigorous selection process that ensures only the finest dry fruits make it to your table.

  • Direct Sourcing: We work directly with farmers, cutting out middlemen to ensure freshness and fair prices.
  • Multi-Stage Inspection: Every batch undergoes visual, texture, moisture, and taste assessments.
  • Premium Storage: Climate-controlled facilities maintain optimal temperature and humidity levels.
  • Innovative Packaging: Vacuum-sealed packages with nitrogen flushing to preserve freshness and extend shelf life naturally.
  • Laboratory Testing: Random samples undergo lab testing for aflatoxins and other contaminants.
