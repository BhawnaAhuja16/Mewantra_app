About Mewantra

Our journey of bringing premium quality dry fruits and nuts to your table with trust, freshness, and exceptional service

Our Story

Mewantra was born from a simple belief: everyone deserves access to premium quality dry fruits and nuts that are fresh, natural, and reasonably priced. What started as a small family business has grown into a trusted name in the dry fruits industry.

We understand that dry fruits are not just snacks - they're an investment in your family's health and well-being. That's why we've dedicated ourselves to sourcing only the finest quality products from trusted suppliers and delivering them fresh to your doorstep.

Every product that carries the Mewantra name has been carefully selected, quality-tested, and packaged with care to ensure you receive nothing but the best.
